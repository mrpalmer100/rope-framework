bundled as both `.docx` and `.pdf`, and mutually consistent. The full corpus is
larger; this is the entry set a reviewer should read, in this order.

## The one-sentence programme

A strong classical, mechanical model of physics (electromagnetism, gravity, optics,
thermodynamics) whose present configuration-counting form demonstrably does not
reproduce quantum entanglement — a boundary documented, not hidden.
